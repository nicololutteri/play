History

The PS2 comes as the next version of the first generation console, aka PS1, in the year 2004 there was a real boom in purchases at the same time as the launch of the most successful game aka San Andreas. Initially it had a rather slow production. The PS2 was unveiled at E3, the largest event dedicated to all videogame fans. It is available in two colors: black or silver. In addition to reading CDs, PS2 had the ability to read video DVDs. Thus becoming a "Home Entertainment" suitable for the whole family. The real strength was the backward compatibility with previous games of the previous one generation.
