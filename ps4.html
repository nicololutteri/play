History

The first viewing of PS4 we have it live in February 2013 from Manhattan in the Playstation Meeting where they come shown some gameplay of the different games that will come out at the launch of the console and the new one controller. On the night between 20 and 21 February, the live stream followed on the Internet began. The official price of the console in Europe is 399 €.

Software

To be able to participate in online games you need the Playstation Plus. Great innovation has it had the graphical interface with the introduction of the Playstation Dynamic Menu which replaces the XMB used by PS3 and PSP. We have backwards compatibility with a new service called PlayStation Now.

Hardware

The controller of the playstation 4 is the DualShock 4, with touch-sensitive surface and the addition share and options buttons.

Single die APU which includes:

4 MByte L2 cache

Unified 64 Shaders shading
Pixel fill rates from 25.6 gigasamples per second

Overall performance declared in floating point: 1.84 ~ 1.86 TeraFLOPS
